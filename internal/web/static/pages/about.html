About Crescendo

Built for people who live for live music.

Crescendo is a discovery platform that connects fans with the artists, concerts, and venues they care about most. We bring tour dates, ticketing, and artist profiles together in one place — so finding your next show feels as good as being there.

Discover

Browse thousands of artists across every genre, and follow the ones you love to stay close to what they're up to.

Plan

See full tour schedules and upcoming dates near you, with real-time on-sale and sold-out status.

Attend

Secure your tickets with confidence — instant delivery, transparent pricing, and a full refund guarantee if a show is cancelled.

Crescendo was built by a small team of music fans who got tired of missing shows because the right information was scattered across a dozen apps. We're just getting started.
