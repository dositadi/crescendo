Privacy Policy

This policy explains what information Crescendo collects, why we collect it, and how you stay in control of it. We've kept it as plain and short as possible.

Last updated: June 1, 2026

On this page

1. Information we collect

When you create a Crescendo account, we collect basic details like your name, email address, and password. When you follow artists or buy tickets, we store that activity so we can show you relevant concerts and keep a record of your purchases.

  • Account details — name, email, password (encrypted)
  • Activity — artists followed, tickets purchased, saved events
  • Payment information — handled directly by our payment processor, never stored on our servers

2. How we use it

We use your information to operate your account, process ticket purchases, send booking confirmations, and recommend artists or concerts that match your interests. We do not use your data to make automated decisions that significantly affect you.

3. Sharing of data

We share the minimum data necessary with trusted partners who help us run Crescendo — for example, our payment processor to complete a purchase, or venue partners to validate your ticket at the door. We never sell your personal data to advertisers.

4. Cookies

Crescendo uses essential cookies to keep you signed in and remember your preferences. We use a small number of analytics cookies to understand how the app is used, which helps us improve it over time.

5. Your rights

You can access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time from your account settings. You may also request a full export of your data or ask us to close your account entirely.

To exercise any of these rights, visit Account Settings → Privacy or reach out via the contact details below.

6. Security

We use industry-standard encryption to protect your data in transit and at rest. Access to personal data within our team is limited to what's needed to provide the service.

7. Contact us

Questions about this policy or how your data is handled? Reach out and we'll be glad to help.
